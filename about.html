Our Mission & Vision

Meet Our Founder

The visionary behind Rivya Ornate

Riya Gupta

Founder & Creative Director

With over 8 years of experience in the fashion and jewelry industry, Riya Gupta founded Rivya Ornate with a simple yet powerful vision: to make elegant jewelry accessible to everyone.

His journey began with a passion for design and a keen eye for detail, which led him to create pieces that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern aesthetics.

"Jewelry is not just an accessory; it's a statement of one's personality. At Rivya Ornate, we believe everyone deserves to shine."

Under Riya leadership, Rivya Ornate has grown from a small startup to a trusted brand serving customers across 25+ countries.
